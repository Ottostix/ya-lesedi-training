# Ya Lesedi Restaurant Development Academy - User Guide

**Website URL:** https://ya-lesedi-training.vercel.app/

**Purpose:** Comprehensive staff training and management platform for restaurant chains and independent establishments across Africa.

**Access:** Login required with role-based access (Master Admin, Store Manager, Staff User)

---

## Powered by Manus

**Technology Stack:**
- **Frontend:** React 19 + TypeScript with Vite for lightning-fast development
- **UI Framework:** Tailwind CSS with shadcn/ui components for accessible interfaces
- **Routing:** Wouter for lightweight client-side navigation
- **Icons & Visualization:** Lucide React for consistent iconography
- **Deployment:** Auto-scaling infrastructure with global CDN on Vercel

This platform delivers enterprise-grade performance with automatic scaling, ensuring your training system remains responsive even as your restaurant network grows.

## Using Your Website

### 1. Logging In
Click "Sign In" and enter your credentials. Demo accounts available:
- **Master Admin:** Username "Tshepo" / Password "2402" (full system access)
- **Staff User:** Username "staff" / Password "password" (limited access)

### 2. Dashboard Overview
After login, you'll see your training hub with:
- **Real-time Statistics:** Total restaurants, active staff, training modules, and completion rates
- **Quick Actions:** Navigate to Users, Quizzes, Menus, or Stores with one click
- **Recent Activity:** Track training progress and staff updates

### 3. Staff Management
Access "Users" to manage your team:
- Click "Add New User" to create staff profiles with roles and permissions
- Search by name or email to find team members quickly
- Edit user details or delete inactive accounts
- Assign roles: Master Admin, Store Manager, or Staff

### 4. Training Quizzes
Navigate to "Quizzes" to create and manage training assessments:
- Click "Create New Quiz" to build custom training modules
- Upload training materials or documents
- Track completion rates and staff performance
- Monitor quiz results and identify training gaps

### 5. Restaurant Documents
Go to "Menus" to manage training materials and documents:
- Click "Upload Document" to add menus, procedures, or training guides
- Organize by category for easy access
- Download documents for offline reference
- Share documents with specific store locations

### 6. Store Analytics
Visit "Stores" to monitor restaurant performance:
- View staff count, training progress, and revenue per location
- Add new restaurant locations with manager assignments
- Track training completion percentages
- Monitor operational metrics across your network

## Managing Your Website

### Settings Panel
Access the **Settings** panel in Management UI to:
- Update website name and logo
- Configure visibility settings (public/private)
- Manage notification preferences
- View and update API credentials

### Database Management
Use the **Database** panel to:
- View and manage user records
- Query training progress data
- Export reports for analysis
- Monitor data integrity

### Dashboard & Analytics
The **Dashboard** panel shows:
- Deployment status and uptime
- Website traffic analytics
- User activity metrics
- Performance monitoring

### Secrets Management
Store sensitive credentials securely in **Settings → Secrets**:
- API keys for external services
- Database connection strings
- Authentication tokens
- Third-party integrations
